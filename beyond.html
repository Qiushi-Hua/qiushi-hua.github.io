Beyond Research

A little about what I do when I’m not thinking about monetary policy — the pastimes that keep me grounded between papers.

✦Cooking

I find cooking deeply meditative — a good excuse to slow down between papers. I especially enjoy making Chinese home-style dishes and experimenting with fermentation.

✦Hiking

I try to get out on long hikes whenever I can. There is nothing like a few miles of trail to clear the mind after a long week of research.

✦Camping

I love camping — setting up a tent, building a fire, and being off-grid for a weekend. National parks are some of my favorite destinations.
